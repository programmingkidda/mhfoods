Malvani chicken Sagoti Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

Ingredients for Marination

  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • 1" piece of Ginger
  • 1 Green Chilli
  • 1/4 cup Coriander Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 lb Boneless Chicken
  • 3/4 cup frozen / fresh Coconut
  • Dash of Nutmeg powder
  • 1 tsp Oil

Ingredients for Malvani Masala

  • 1 tsp Shahi Jeera
  • Whole Spices - 2 Bay Leafs, Star anise, Cinnamon Stick, 3 Cloves, 3 Black Peppercorns, 1 Javitri
  • 1 Big Onion sliced
  • 1 Onion finely chopped

Ingredients for Malvani Curry

  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 1/2 tsp Degi Mirch powder

INSTRUCTIONS

Marination

  1. Grind garlic, green chilli, ginger, lemon juice, coriander leaves, and salt together into a blender.
  2. Apply this green chutney over chicken pieces really well.
  3. Marinate this for 1 hour.

Malvani Masala

  1. Heat up 1 tsp oil, to that add shahi jeera, whole spices, and cook for a minute.
  2. Add sliced onion and cook till onion gets light brown color.
  3. Add frozen coconut and continue cooking till coconut gets light brown color.
  4. Add a dash of nutmeg, mix, and wait till it becomes cool.
  5. Once masala cools down, blend to make a smooth puree.

Malavni Curry

  1. Heat up oil, add chopped onion and cook till it gets a light golden color.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add coriander powder, garam masala, red chilli powder and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add marinated chicken pieces, mix well, add water, salt, cover and cook till chicken gets cooked.
  5. Add the ground onion, coconut masala, mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with Malvani vade or Rice flour bhakri.
