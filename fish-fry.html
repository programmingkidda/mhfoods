Fish FryRecipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 lb Fish
  • 2 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp of Finely Chopped Green Chilies
  • 1 tsp Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Chopped Coriander Leaves
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • Salt to Taste
  • 2 tbsp Rice Flour
  • 2 tbsp Sooji or Rava

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash and pat dry fish.
  2. Cut fish in about 2" long cubes.
  3. Now take ginger garlic paste, green chili, vinegar, coriander leaves, salt into mixing bowl.
  4. Put fish pieces in the above marinade, mix fish very well.
  5. Cover and allow it to rest for 10 mts.
  6. Fish Fry preparation
  7. Now take all dry masala into a bowl.
  8. Mix turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, chili powder, garam masala, salt.
  9. Mix the above ingredients very well.
  10. Now in a separate dish mix rice flour and sooji well.
  11. Now take marinated fish and rub it well in dry masala powder. Give that a good coating of masala.
  12. Then again coat masala fish in sooji and flour mixture.
  13. Heat oil in a pan, and shallow or deep fry fish till it gets a good golden color.
  14. Serve hot with mint chutney!
