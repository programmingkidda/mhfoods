Gavran Mataki Rassa Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 cup raw Peanuts
  • 2 tbsp shredded dry Coconut
  • 10~12 Garlic cloves
  • Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  • A pinch of Hing
  • 10~12 Curry leaves
  • Finely chopped Onion
  • Finely chopped Tomato
  • 1 tsp Kala masala
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • Sprouted Mataki
  • Water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp Gudh / Jaggery (Optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Roast peanuts on medium heat a little and add coconut, garlic.
  2. Fry it a little for another 2 minutes.
  3. When coconut gets light golden color turn off the gas and let it cool down a little.
  4. Transfer everything into a blender jar and blend it into powder.
  5. Basic masala for mataki rassa is ready.
  6. Heat up oil in a pan.
  7. Add mustard seeds and let them pop up.
  8. Add cumin seeds and let it splutter.
  9. Add hing, curry leaves, onion and fry until onion gets nice golden color.
  10. When onion gets light golden color add tomato and fry everything for about 2-3 minutes.
  11. When onion and tomato cook soft add the blended masala, kala masala, red chili powder and mix well.
  12. You can use goda masala, kanda lasun masala or any other masala that is stored with you in place of kala masala.
  13. When masala is fried well add mataki and mix well.
  14. Add water, salt and mix well.
  15. Bring it to boil. When rassa begins to boil turn the heat to medium, cover and cook for about 15-20 minutes.
  16. When mataki is cooked well add jaggery and mix well.
  17. Adding gudh is optional. You can skip it if you don’t want.
  18. Gavran mataki rassa is all ready.
  19. You can have it with hot bhakari oo chapatti.
