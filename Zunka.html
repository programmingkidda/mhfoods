ZunkaRecipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Besan
  • 1 big finely chopped Onion
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
  • A pinch of Hing
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped Garlic
  • 2 tsp Oil
  • Finely chopped Coriander leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat up oil in a pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them pop up.
  3. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add hing and garlic. Mix well and fry for about 30-40 seconds.
  5. Add onion and fry it well.
  6. Add red chili powder and mix well.
  7. Add besan and mix well.
  8. Fry besan for about 2-3 minutes to remove the rawness of it.
  9. Add hot water, salt and mix well quickly.
  10. Cover and seam on low to low to medium heat for about 7-8 minutes.
  11. Turn off the gas and add coriander leaves.
  12. Mix well and zunka is already.
  13. Zunka goes well with bhakari, poli, fulka or rice too.
