Tandoor Chicken Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 2.5 lb Whole Chicken
  • 2 tbsp Degi Mirch powder
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Degi Mirch Powder
  • 1 tbsp Kasoori Methi
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Roasted Cumin powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Corn Flour for dusting

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash off whole chicken inside out and then pat dry using a paper towel.
  2. Make gashes on all over the chicken using a knife.
  3. Mix red chili powder, lemon juice, salt, ginger-garlic paste, oil, mix and insert masala inside gashes as well as an inside cavity.
  4. Keep it to rest for 15 mts.
  5. Take thick yogurt, to that add red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, kasoori methi, salt, oil, mix well. Add food color if desired.
  6. Now apply this tandoori masala paste well on all over the chicken.
  7. Cover and keep it to marinate in the refrigerator overnight.
  8. Remove chicken 30 minutes before you want to bake it.
  9. Dust cornflour using tea strainer all over the chicken.
  10. Brush some oil on the top of it.
  11. Take a Baking tray, place a cooling or grill rack on top of it. Put the chicken on the top of the cooling rack.
  12. Bake at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 25 mts.
  13. After 25 mts flip the chicken and again bake for 20 more mts.
  14. After 20 mts again flip the chicken one more time and bake for the final 20 mts.
  15. Serve hot with salad and wedges of lemon on the side.
