Vegetable Korma Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup Cauliflower florets
  • Finely chopped Beans
  • Sliced Carrot
  • Fresh peas
  • Chopped Potatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded fresh Coconut
  • 1~2 dried Red chilies
  • 4 Black peppercorns
  • 2~3 Cloves
  • A small piece of Cinnamon
  • Oil
  • 2 Cinnamon sticks
  • 2 Cloves
  • 2 Black peppercorns
  • Finely chopped Onion
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Few Cashew nuts
  • Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Take a cooker and add water at the base.
  2. Take flower, carrot, beans, carrot, peas, potatoes in a cooker pot.
  3. You can use veggies of your choice.
  4. Add water and close the lid.
  5. Cook on medium heat until just 1 whistle.
  6. Take fresh coconut, dry red chilies, black peppercorns, cloves, cinnamon, a little water in a blender jar and blend everything into a smooth paste.
  7. You can use dry coconut in place of fresh coconut.
  8. The masala is all-ready.
  9. Heat up oil in a pan. Add cinnamon, cloves, black pepper, onion, and fry until onion gets a light golden color.
  10. Add the blended masala, coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and mix well.
  11. Fry everything for about 2-3 minutes.
  12. Add the cooked veggies, cashew nuts, salt and mix everything well together.
  13. Add water to adjust the consistency and simmer the korma for about 8-10 minutes.
  14. Veg korma is all-ready.
  15. It thickens up as time passes. So decide what consistency you want.
  16. This korma goes well with puri or bun.
