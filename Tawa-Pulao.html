Tawa PulaoRecipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 cup Onion finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/4 cup Capsicum
  • 1/4 cup Carrot diced
  • 1/4 cup French Beans
  • 1/2 cup Tomato diced
  • 1/4 cup Green Peas
  • 1 tsp Pav Bhaji Masala
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups Boiled Rice
  • Finely chopped Coriander leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat up butter in a pan.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add finely chopped onion and fry it well for about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and mix well.
  5. Add capsicum, carrot, french beans and fry it for about 4-5 minutes.
  6. Add tomato, green peas and cook everything well for about 3-4 minutes.
  7. Mash the veggies a little.
  8. Add half of pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, salt and mix well.
  9. Add rice, left over masala, red chili powder, salt and mix well.
  10. Add salt carefully if you have added salt while cooking the rice.
  11. Fry the rice well on medium heat for about 4-5 minutes mixing continuously.
  12. Add finely chopped coriander leaves and mix well.
  13. Tawa pulao is already.
  14. You can have tawa pulao as it is or you can serve it with raita too.
