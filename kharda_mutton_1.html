INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tsp Oil
  • Finely chopped Onion
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 500 gm Mutton
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded fresh Coconut
  • 8~9 Green chilies
  • 10~12 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp white Sesame seeds
  • 1” finely chopped Ginger
  • Coriander leaves
  • 2 tsp Oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat up oil in a cooker and add onion
  2. Fry until onion turns nice golden.
  3. When onion turns nice golden add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder and fry for about a minute
  4. .Add mutton and fry it well with masala.
  5. When mutton gets nice coating of masala add a little water and mix well.
  6. Add salt and mix well.
  7. Close the lid and cook on medium heat until 7-8 whistles.
  8. For making vatan take fresh coconut, green chilies, garlic, white sesame seeds, ginger, coriander leaves in a blender jar
  9. Blend everything together into smooth paste. You can add water if needed.
  10. Green masala or vatan is ready.
  11. Heat up oil in a pan and add vatan in it.
  12. Fry it for about 6-7 minutes.
  13. When the masala dries up add garam masala and mix well.
  14. Add the mutton stock first and cook until the masala begins to release oil.
  15. dd salt and mix well.
  16. Add cooked mutton and mix well.
  17. Cook everything well together for another 7-8 minutes. No need to cover it with lid.
