Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tsp Oil
  • 1 tsp finely chopped Garlic
  • Sliced Onion
  • Finely chopped Carrot
  • Finely chopped Beans
  • Shredded Cabbage
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Soya sauce
  • 1 tsp Tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp Red chili paste
  • 1 tsp Black pepper powder
  • Cooked rice
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil
  • 1 Egg
  • A pinch of Salt
  • A pinch of Black pepper powder

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat up oil in a pan.
  2. Add garlic, onion, carrot, beans and fry for just 2 minutes.
  3. Do not overcook the veggies. They should retain their crunch.
  4. Add cabbage and mix well.
  5. Move the veggies aside in the pan and break eggs in the remaining space.
  6. Mix well and cook the eggs for just 2 minutes.
  7. Mix eggs and veggies well together.
  8. Add soya sauce, tomato sauce, red chili paste, black pepper powder and mix well.
  9. Add cooked rice, salt and mix well.
  10. Fry for a few minutes.
  11. Egg fried rice is already.
  12. Heat up oil in a pan.
  13. Break an egg and sprinkle salt, black pepper powder.
  14. Cover and cook for a few minutes.
  15. When omelet is cooked well transfer it on the fried rice and serve.
