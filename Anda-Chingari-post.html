Anda Chingari Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tsp Oil
  • Finely chopped Onion
  • Finely chopped Tomato
  • Ginger paste
  • 2 Eggs
  • Garam masala
  • Coriander powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Tomato puree
  • Water
  • Chopped boiled Egg
  • Finely chopped Coriander leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat up oil in a pan.
  2. Add onion and fry until it turns light golden.
  3. Add tomato, ginger paste and mix well.
  4. Fry for a few seconds and move the masala towards the edge of the pan.
  5. Break 2 eggs and mix egg white and egg yellow well.
  6. Mix everything with onion and tomato.
  7. Add garam masala, coriander powder, red chili powder, salt and mix well.
  8. Add tomato puree and mix well.
  9. Add a little water and mix well.
  10. Add boiled egg and a little water again.
  11. Mix well and add coriander leaves.
  12. Mix well and anda chingari is all ready.
  13. Serve with ladi pav and onion.
  14. Garnish with boiled egg and finely chopped coriander leaves.
