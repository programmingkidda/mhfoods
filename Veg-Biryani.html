Veg Biryani Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

    For Biryani Masala:

  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 2~3 Star anise
  • 1 tsp Stone flower
  • 4~5 Cloves
  • 4~5 Peppercorns
  • 1 Mace
  • 1 piece of Cinnamon
  • 1 Black cardamom
  • 2 Green cardamom
  • 1~2 Bay leaves

    • For Cooking Rice:

  • 1~2 bay leaves
  • 1 Mace
  • 2 Star anise
  • 2 Green cardamom
  • A little Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 3~4 Peppercorns
  • 3~4 Cloves
  • 1 1/2 cup Long grain basmati rice

    • For Marinating Vegetables:

  • Sliced Carrot
  • Chopped Green beans
  • 1 Potato peeled & chopped into big chunks
  • Paneer chopped into big chunks
  • 1/4 cup Green peas
  • Cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup Curd
  • Finely chopped Coriander leaves
  • Few Mint leaves
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Biryani masala
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 slit green chilies

    • For Making Biryani:

  • Finely chopped Onion
  • Ghee
  • Finely chopped Tomato
  • 1/4 cup Fried Onion
  • Finely chopped Coriander leaves
  • Few Mint leaves
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Salt Few Saffron strands
  • Oil

INSTRUCTIONS

    For Biryani Masala:

  1. Take bay leaves into a blender jar.
  2. Add stone flower, cinnamon, peppercorns, cloves, star anise, mace, Black cardamom, green cardamom cumin seeds, and blend it into a coarse powder.
  3. Do not need to roast the spices. Biryani masala is already.

    4. For Marinating Vegetables:

  4. Take curd into a bowl. Add red chili powder, coriander powder, ginger-garlic paste, salt and mix everything well together.
  5. Add 1 tsp biryani masala, a few hand-crushed mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chili and mix well.
  6. Add potato, cauliflower, carrot, green beans, green peas and mix well.
  7. Veggies should be coated with the marinade.
  8. Cover and rest for about 30 minutes.

    9. For Making Rice:

  9. Wash the rice really good 2-3 times with water.
  10. Soak rice in enough water for about 30 minutes.
  11. Boil water in a pan.
  12. Add 1 tbsp salt, bay leaves, mace, star anise, cinnamon, green cardamom, cloves peppercorns, cumin seed, 2 tsp ghee, and soaked rice.
  13. Ghee gives a nice flavor to biryani and rice doesn’t become sticky.
  14. Cook rice until it is 70-80% cooked for about 7-8 minutes. Do not cook the rice completely.

    15. For Making Biryani:

  15. Heat up oil in a pan.
  16. Add onion and fry it really good until it gets a nice golden color.
  17. Add tomato and fry it for about 4-5 minutes until it is mashed well with onion.
  18. Add ginger-garlic paste, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, a little salt, 1 tsp biryani masala, coriander leaves, hand crushed mint leaves, and mix well.
  19. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  20. Add marinated veggies and mix well. Veggies should get a nice coating of masala.
  21. Add 2-3 tbsp water and mix well.
  22. Cover and cook on medium heat until these are 60-70% cooked for about 10 minutes. Do not cook the veggies completely.
  23. After 10 minutes add paneer and mix well.
  24. Drain the water from rice and give a layer of it on veggies.
  25. After layering the rice add saffron water. Saffron gives a nice flavor and color.
  26. Add a lot of ghee. Add fried onion, finely chopped coriander leaves, few mints leaves.
  27. Add water in which we cooked rice in the biryani so it gets enough moisture.
  28. Cover and cook on high heat means 130 Deg C on induction for about 5 minutes.
  29. Cook biryani on medium heat means 100 Deg C for 10 minutes more.
  30. Cook on lowest possible heat means 80 Deg C on induction for about 20 minutes.
  31. Biryani is already.
  32. You can garnish the biryani with few fried cashew nuts and raisins if you want.
  33. This biryani will serve for about 4-5 people.
