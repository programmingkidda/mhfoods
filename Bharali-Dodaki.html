Bharali Dodaki Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup raw Peanuts
  • Shredded dry Coconut
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp white Sesame seeds (Optional)
  • Curry leaves
  • 7~8 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp Kala masala
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • Coriander leaves
  • A little Water
  • Dodaka / Ridge gourd
  • 3 tsp Oil
  • Water

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat up a pan on medium heat and add peanuts.
  2. Roast peanuts until light brown.
  3. When peanuts begin to change the color add coconut, cumin seeds, white sesame seeds, curry leaves, garlic and fry for about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Adding sesame seeds is optional. You can skip it if you don’t want.
  5. When everything is roasted well turn off the gas and let it cool down.
  6. Transfer the mixture into a blender jar and add kala masala, red chili powder, salt, sugar, coriander leaves
  7. Blend it into powder and masala id ready.
  8. Transfer the masala into a dish and add water to give binding and mix well.
  9. Cut off the end portions of ridge gourd and peel it.
  10. Cut it into 2” pieces and give it a slit at the center like we do for bharala vange.
  11. You can give 2 cuts if want like vange.
  12. Stuff the masala in each piece of gourd.
  13. Heat up oil in a pan. Drop the stuffed pieces into it and cover it to cook.
  14. After steaming for about 3-4 minutes remove the lid and flip the pieces over.
  15. Cover and steam for maximum 2-3 minutes more.
  16. After about 2 minutes remove the lid and mix well once.
  17. If you want to make gravy, move the ridge gourd aside and add the remaining masala into the oil.
  18. Fry the masala for 2 minutes.
  19. When the masala is roasted well mix it with ridge gourd and add water.
  20. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  21. Bharala dodaka is all ready.
