Paneer Biryani Recipe

Back Next

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups whole Basmati rice
  • Water
  • 1 tsp Oil
  • Few Cashew nuts
  • Dry sliced Onion
  • 1 cup Curd
  • 2~3 slit Green chilies
  • 1 tsp Biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Kasuri methi
  • Finely chopped Coriander leaves
  • Few Mint leaves
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Oil
  • 1/2 kg Paneer cubes
  • 2 tsp Oil
  • 2 tsp Ghee
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  • Finely chopped Onion
  • Finely chopped Tomato
  • 1 tsp Biryani masala
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • Slit Green chilies
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Water
  • Finely chopped Coriander leaves
  • Mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water
  • 1 tap Ghee
  • 3~4 Cloves
  • 3~4 Black peppercorns
  • 3~4 Bay leaves
  • 2 Star anise
  • 2~3 Cinnamon sticks
  • 2 Black cardamom
  • Salt
  • Ghee
  • 2~3 tsp Ghee
  • Mint leaves
  • Finely chopped Coriander leaves
  • Saffron milk

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Take basmati into a bowl and wash it well 3-4 times.
  2. Soak it in enough water for about 30 minutes.
  3. Heat up oil in a pan.
  4. Add cashew nuts and fry it well until it gets a light golden color.
  5. Take the cashew nuts out into a dish.
  6. In the same oil add dry sliced onion and fry for just about 2 minutes.
  7. You can rub oil to dry onion and microwave it for just a minute.
  8. Take curd into a bowl.
  9. Add green chilies, biryani masala, turmeric powder, coriander powder, salt, Kasuri methi, coriander leaves, mint leaves, fried onion, red chili powder and mix everything well together.
  10. Add oil and mix well.
  11. Add paneer cubes and coat it well with spices.
  12. Cover and rest it for about 30 minutes to marinate.
  13. Heat up 2 tsp oil in a pan.
  14. Add ghee, cumin seeds and let it splutter.
  15. Add onion and fry until it gets a light golden color.
  16. Add tomato, biryani masala, coriander powder, turmeric powder, green chili, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder and mix well.
  17. Fry for about 3-4 minutes really good.
  18. Add water, coriander leaves, mint leaves, and mix well.
  19. Add salt to taste and cook the gravy for just about 2-3 minutes. Keep the pan aside.
  20. Boil water in a pan.
  21. Add ghee, cloves, black peppercorns, bay leaves, star anise, cinnamon, black cardamom, salt, soaked rice & cook until rice is about 70% cooked, for about 7-8 minutes.
  22. When rice gets cooked about 60-70% drain the water from it.
  23. Give a layer of cooked rice into the gravy pan.
  24. Add ghee and layer of marinated paneer.
  25. Again give a layer of remaining rice on the paneer layer.
  26. Add ghee again, mint leaves, coriander leaves, fried onion, saffron milk, fried cashew nuts, and water in which we boiled the rice.
  27. Cover with lid. Don’t close the lid of the cooker & don’t remove the whistle.
  28. Cook on medium heat for about 5 minutes.
  29. Lower the heat to low and cook on low heat for about 30 minutes.
  30. Paneer biryani is all-ready.
